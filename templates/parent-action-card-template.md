# Parent Action Card Template

Use this compact template at the end of a BabyGear Risk Radar report.

## Do Now

- `[Inspect product condition, age fit, and attachment setup.]`
- `[Remove or stop using damaged products or unsafe strings/cords/attachments.]`
- `[Record product name, model, batch/date code, purchase country, and packaging claims.]`

## Check Next

- `[Official recall portal or regulator source.]`
- `[Manufacturer batch-specific support response or certificate.]`
- `[Primary test report or lab method, if public reporting is disputed.]`
- `[Pediatric or clinician guidance for child-specific concerns.]`

## Compare Alternatives By

- `[Material disclosure and chemical-claim scope.]`
- `[Physical safety design and age fit.]`
- `[Traceability: model, batch, date code, certificates.]`
- `[Cleaning, replacement schedule, and deterioration checks.]`
- `[Recall history and official guidance in the purchase jurisdiction.]`

## Escalate If

- `[An official recall or product safety warning matches the product.]`
- `[The product is damaged, degraded, separating, or has unsafe attachments.]`
- `[The child has symptoms, special vulnerability, or the parent needs medical interpretation.]`
- `[The manufacturer cannot answer batch-specific safety questions.]`

## Boundary

This card does not diagnose, prescribe, or declare a real product safe or dangerous. It helps organize next checks and escalation paths.
