# Avent Pacifier Risk Brief

## Case Boundary

This is a public-source case note for testing BabyGear Risk Radar. It does not state that a real product is safe or unsafe.

## Product Context

- Product candidate: Philips Avent Ultra Air soother / pacifier.
- Use case: newborn or infant soothing, including possible nap or bedtime use.
- Parent concern: a trusted-brand BPA-free claim appears to conflict with public reporting about an alleged consumer-test detection, while the manufacturer disputes the result.

## Parent Decision Need

The parent wants a calm process for deciding whether to continue use, pause and replace, seek official confirmation, or consult a pediatrician/regulator.

## Alternative Criteria

- Official recall status.
- Batch and tracking-label clarity.
- Material disclosure.
- Independent or third-party testing availability.
- Physical safety: one-piece construction, shield size, ventilation, no cords or attachments.
- Cleaning instructions suitable for the child's age.
- Manufacturer responsiveness and source transparency.
